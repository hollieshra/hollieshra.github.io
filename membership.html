Membership

The Hollies Residents Association (HRA) exists to:

  • Keep The Hollies an attractive, peaceful and desirable place to live
  • Represent the views and interests of residents of The Hollies to bodies such as Countryside Properties PLC and LB Bexley Council.
  • Communicate news of interest to residents
  • Organise social events for residents

Please note that the Hollies Residents Association is a different entity to the HRA Management Company (Kent) Ltd. (aka "Manco"); the latter is the company which runs the leisure centre at The Hollies (see link above). Any enquiries regarding the leisure centre should be addressed to them.

We ask residents for a membership fee of £4 per year to cover our expenses. Please join or renew your membership online using the PayPal button below to pay £4 by debit/credit card or using a PayPal account if you have one.
